General

Features

We analyze token contracts and simulate buy/sell transactions to detect if a token blocks selling, has hidden taxes, or uses other honeypot techniques. We also check transaction history for suspicious patterns like no sells despite many buys.
If your wallet is compromised (leaked private key, phishing attack, etc.), Emergency Evacuation instantly transfers ALL your assets to a safe wallet in under 3 seconds. This can save your funds before an attacker drains them.
We're the first to track DPRK (North Korean) state-sponsored hackers on Solana. The Lazarus Group has stolen billions in crypto. We monitor known wallets and campaign patterns to protect users from state-level threats.
